What Is A Plant?

A Plant is a living organism of the kind exemplified by trees, shrubs, herbs, grasses, ferns, grasses, ferns, and mosses, typically gorwing in a permanent site, absorbing water and inorganic substances through its root, and synthesizing nutrients in its leaves by photosynthesis using the green pigment chlorophyll.
